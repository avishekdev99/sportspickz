The F50 Returns

This summer, Adidas is reviving the iconic F50 football boots, replacing the Adidas X series. Official images of the regular launch edition of the Adidas F50 football boots have now been leaked.
Read More

Adidas F50 2024 'Advancement' Pack - Copa America/Euro 2024 - White/Solar Red/Lucid Blue The new Adidas F50 Speed Legacy football shoes in 'White/Solar Red/Lucid Blue' feature a modern design. The 2024 Copa America/Euro 2024 Adidas F50 'Advancement Pack' boasts a white upper with red and blue logos and accents. This color scheme is part of a streamlined pack for the tournaments, with the Adidas Copa and Predator following similar themesâ€”blue for the Predator and predominantly white for the Copa.

Inspired by the classic Adidas F50, the 2024 boots include short horizontal Three Stripes on the outstep and large F50 branding for a contemporary look.

Adidas F50 Plus The Adidas F50 Plus soccer cleats are the premium version, featuring a more dynamic design and Lightstrike Pro technology in the forefoot area for enhanced performance.

Adidas F50 Elite Laceless These soccer cleats mirror the Plus version but without the Lightstrike Pro element.

Adidas F50 Elite Laces This version includes a Compression Fit Tunnel Tongue for improved lockdown and speed, catering to players who prefer laces.

Adidas F50 Elite Mid While images of the Elite Mid boots in the Advancement Pack are not yet available, they feature a mid-cut collar inspired by the Nike Mercurial Superfly boots. Developed with insights from top women's players, these boots are suitable for both men and women.

The new F50 football boots are based on previous Adidas X models, with an upper similar to the Adidas Speedflow.

Adidas F50 'Advancement' - Features
  • Successor of Adidas X; reintroduction of F50
  • Four top versions: Plus, Elite Laceless, Elite Laces, and Elite Mid (developed with women's players' insights)
  • Debut in the 2024 Champions League final, 2024 Copa America, and Euro 2024
  • Thin Fibertouch upper for enhanced ball control
  • Compression Fit Tunnel Tongue (laces only) for secure fit
  • Innovative Sprintweb 3D texture for improved ball handling
  • Springy Sprintframe 360 for agility
  • Lightstrike Pro foam (Plus version only) for energy return
  • Price: around $280 USD
  • Colorway: White/Solar Red/Lucid Blue
  • Release date: June 3, 2024

The new F50 'Advancement' Pack soccer boots from Adidas will be available starting Monday, June 3, 2024, priced at approximately $280 USD.
