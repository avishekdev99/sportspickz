Real Madrid Reveal 24-25 Shirt

Here’s everything you need to know about what Real Madrid will be sporting next season.

Real Madrid's partnership with Adidas, which began in 1998, has produced numerous iconic kits for the La Liga giants. Their pristine Blanco home shirts are among football’s most recognizable images, and their away kits, worn by legends like Cristiano Ronaldo and Gareth Bale, have also left a lasting impression.

Read More

Adidas unveiled the Real Madrid 2024-25 home kit on June 5, featuring the team's traditional white color accented with black for the Three Stripes.

Here’s a closer look at what stars like Kylian Mbappe, Vinicius Jr, and Jude Bellingham will be wearing for Real Madrid in the 2024-25 season, including the latest leaks, prices, and where to purchase them.

Real Madrid 2024-25 Home Kit, Release Date & Price

Adidas has delivered another classic design for the 2024-25 season, with a return to black shoulder stripes and a contrasting white-black-white collar. This year's jersey also features a unique houndstooth pattern incorporating the initials 'RM' throughout the fabric, adding texture and paying homage to Real Madrid's storied history. The long-sleeved version extends the shoulder stripes from the elbow down to the cuff.

The Real Madrid 2024-25 home kit is now available for purchase through Real Madrid CF stores, selected Adidas retailers, and the club’s online store. The men's authentic shirt is priced at $292, while the replica costs $199.

Real Madrid 2024-25 Away Kit, Release Date & Price

According to social media leaks, Real Madrid will don an orange kit for their away games in the 2024-25 season, reminiscent of the 2013-14 third kit. The rumored design features a solid orange base with black shoulder stripes and a round neck collar framed in black and white.

Real Madrid 2024-25 Third Kit, Release Date & Price

Leaks from 'Opaleak' suggest that the third kit for 2024-25 will showcase a grey, black, and white colorway. This kit will include a polo neck collar and the Adidas trefoil logo from the Originals range. The 'RMCF' from the club badge will be subtly embossed on the shirt, shimmering under light.

Real Madrid 2024-25 Goalkeeper Kit, Release Date & Price

While the goalkeeper kits have not been officially released, it’s expected that there will be three different designs, corresponding to each outfield kit. Based on last year’s colors (green, black, and red), the 2024-25 range is expected to feature blue-pink, green-yellow, and black-red designs.
